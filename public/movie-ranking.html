The Best Movies According to Hitesh

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

Interstellar

The movie is about a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole to find a new planet for humanity to inhabit. The story is fictional, but it incorporates scientific concepts. The movie is about Earth's last chance to find a habitable planet before the human race goes extinct.

La La Land

La La Land is a 2016 American musical romance film. It tells the story of a struggling jazz pianist and an aspiring actress who meet and fall in love while pursuing their dreams in Los Angeles. The film stars Ryan Gosling and Emma Stone, and also features J.K. Simmons.

Shutter Island

The film is about two US Marshals, Teddy Daniels and Chuck Aule, who are sent to a remote island to investigate the disappearance of a patient. Teddy uncovers a shocking truth about the place.
